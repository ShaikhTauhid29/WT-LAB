Dr. Geetika Narang
is an accomplished academician currently serving as an Associate Professor and Head of Department (HOD). She holds a Ph.D. and has an extensive 17 years of teaching experience. Her expertise lies in the fields of Networking and Data Mining, where she has contributed significantly to research and education. With a strong commitment to academic excellence, she plays a pivotal role in mentoring students and advancing technological knowledge. She can be reached at geetikanarang.tcoer@kjei.edu.in for academic inquiries and collaborations.
